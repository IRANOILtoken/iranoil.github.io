IRANOIL Roadmap

IRANOIL Token is a utility token built on the TON blockchain, inspired by the strategic value of crude oil. With a fixed supply of 20,000,000,000 tokens and 9 decimals, IRANOIL aims to provide a transparent, fast, and secure foundation for digital transactions, investments, and energy-related Web3 projects.

The project focuses on:

  • Strong community building
  • Fair distribution and long-term sustainability
  • Gradual liquidity growth on TON-based DEXs
  • Future integration with global trade and financial ecosystems

IRANOIL represents innovation, trust, and accessibility in the evolving blockchain landscape.
